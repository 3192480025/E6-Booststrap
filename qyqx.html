Spirited Away

Description

Spirited Away tells the story of Chihiro, a young girl who finds herself trapped in a mysterious and magical world of spirits. To return to the human world, she must work in a bathhouse for the spirits and find her inner strength.

Main Cast

Chihiro - Voiced by Rumi Hiragi

Haku - Voiced by Miyu Irino

Yubaba - Voiced by Mari Natsuki

Kamaji - Voiced by Bunta Sugawara

Trivia

It is the only non-English language film to win the Academy Award for Best Animated Feature.

The movie was made without a script; Hayao Miyazaki wrote the story as he drew the storyboard!

Spirited Away overtook Titanic's box office record in Japan when released.
